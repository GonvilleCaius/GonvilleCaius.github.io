Bateman Auditorium Instructions

 

1 At the back of the room next to the glass box are several switches that control the projector and the screen. One of them turns power on for the projector and another two bring the screen up and down, these switches must be held. Each of the switches is labelled to avoid confusion.

2 Below the Lecturn is a white plug, this powers all the devices within the Lecturn. The Audio equipment, microphone, and laptop connection all require this plug to be turned on.

3 Turn on the specific equipment you will be using within the Lecturn. For Example, if you require audio the amp must be turned on.

4 The AMP is the first device within the rack, it has all dials labelled to help users control the volume of devices.

5 The Lecturn itself has a control panel built into the top half. This control panel places the projector into standby mode and is the preferred method for controlling.

Laptop

1 The laptop should be left on the Lecturn, it will only have a power lead.

2 The account on the laptop is named Conference and does not have a password, this account should have admin credentials.

3 You should not leave any personal data on this laptop as it is for public use.

 

Microphones

1 We have two handheld microphones and two radio microphones; these are kept at the main porter’s lodge. If you require them simply ask.

2 The receivers for the microphones are the bottom rack and are labelled as such. HAND 1, HAND 2.

3 They must be powered on to begin working.

 

Power Off

After use bring up the screen and power off the Lecturn and the projector, many thanks.
